Survey Gun

Another MERN (Mongo, Express, ReactJs, NodeJS) stack app built with the purpose of improving my skills in the stack. It has an authentication system based on the Google oauth flow. This means, clients are able to login using their Google account. Here, clients are supposed to be business owners who can use the app to send survey emails to their users asking for useful feedback to help them improve their business. The app has a stripe integration allowing clients to purchase credits they need to send surveys

Squeak Camp

A NodeJS Express application for cataloging camping sites. It features a sessions/cookies based authentication system. Registered users can post info about camping sites, see all camping sites posted by other users, and comment on the details page for a camping site. The app also has Google map integration, so that a camping site's location can be pin-point on the map. The purpose of this website was to gain more experience with NodeJS, MongoDB, and Express

Simon Game

If he gets the sequence right, he moves to the next round. The first round has just one press, the second round adds one more button press, and so on. If the player gets all sequences right for 20 rounds (the 20th round will have a sequence of 20 button presses!), he wins.{" "}
